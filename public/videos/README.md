Add your demo videos to this directory with the following filenames:

1. **lesson-plan-demo.mp4** - AI Lesson Plan Generator demo video
2. **student-management-demo.mp4** - Student Management demo video
3. **hope-ai-demo.mp4** - Hope AI Assistant demo video

## Required Poster Images:

1. **lesson-plan-poster.jpg** - Poster/thumbnail for AI Lesson Plan Generator video
2. **student-management-poster.jpg** - Poster/thumbnail for Student Management video
3. **hope-ai-poster.jpg** - Poster/thumbnail for Hope AI Assistant video

## Video Specifications:

- **Format**: MP4 (H.264 codec recommended)
- **Resolution**: 1920x1080 (Full HD) or higher
- **Duration**: 2-5 minutes recommended
- **File Size**: Keep under 50MB for web performance
- **Aspect Ratio**: 16:9 recommended

## Poster Image Specifications:

- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **Resolution**: 1920x1080 (Full HD) or higher
- **Aspect Ratio**: 16:9 (same as video)
- **File Size**: Keep under 2MB for fast loading
- **Content**: Should be a representative frame from the video or a custom thumbnail
